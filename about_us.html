About us

We are the YALiEns! Our team was started by Matthew Brady, a former math teacher at Y.A.L.E. School. It is now ran by Mrs. Murray, a science teacher at our school. Our team is open to our school's middle and high school students. 

Our rookie year was 2012, where we had our first competitions. Since then, new members have joined, and little knowledge of that year is known. We do know that our team has grown and will continue to grow. The knowledge that we have learned will be passed down to the new members, and they will be able to keep our team living on.

This website is a student based website. Please contact the coach for comments or questions.
