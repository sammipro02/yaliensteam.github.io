FLASHBACK! #001 - "The New 3D Printer" (Part 1)
-- Before we begin, this is the first in a series of flashbacks to past experiences on the robotics team. This series will serve as a medium to tell future members about what happened on the team in the past. Now, let's get into the story.

This was a very exciting day for me. I was walking to gym class, and on the way there, I saw a box for a new 3D Printer box in the hallway of the school where all the other packages were. I was so excited to tell Mrs. Murray that it arrived.

I believe it was either lunch or dismissal, and I visited her room to tell her about it. We walked back to the area where the deliveries were, and the office was right there, so we asked the people in the office if we could take the 3D Printer to the classroom. They said our computer company had to bring it to the class. So we went all the way back to the upper building to get someone from the computer company to bring it over. So he went over and fetched it for us. Unfortunately, it was the end of the day, so we couldn't unbox it yet, so we had to wait until another time to do so.

Summary of the move to our new facility

Recently, we just moved to a new Y.A.L.E. School facility. It is a much larger room than what we had previously. Before, we had a science classroom at the Cherry Hill campus. Now we have a large lab that we can fit a full test field on. 

The space is much more than what we expected, and will be very useful for all of the things we need. There are a few things that need attention in the room, but they are already being addressed.
